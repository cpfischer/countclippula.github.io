About Count Clippula

The story behind our hauntingly good salon

Our Story

Count Clippula was born from a passion for creative expression and a desire to create a salon where everyone feels welcome. Founded in 2020, our salon combines expert styling with a playful, nostalgic atmosphere that celebrates individuality.

What started as a small dream has grown into a beloved community space where clients can express their authentic selves through their hair. Our team of diverse stylists brings a range of perspectives and techniques to create looks that are as unique as you are.
